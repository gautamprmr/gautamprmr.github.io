Pacific National Exhibition

Pacific National Exhibition is an amusement park located in Hasting Park in Vancouver, British Columbia Canada. PNE also reffered to Playland is the oldest amusement park in Canada. A lot of famous films such as Five People You Meet in Heaven was shot at this park.

Prices

  • Admission for limited rides $29.50
  • Admission for thrill rides 48" plus $39.50
  • 3 year olds and younger is free with paying adult
There are three roller coasters in Playland. This is the Wooden Rollercoaster and has been up and running since 1958 making this Canadas oldest roller coaster. The Wooden Rollercoaster hits top speed of 80km/h. There are tons of rides such as elevator, beach party etc...
